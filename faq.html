Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VARNWEAR

How do I track my order?

+
You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the Orders page. You'll find tracking information for all your orders there. You'll also receive tracking updates via email.

Can I cancel my order?

+
Yes, you can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Go to your Orders page and click the Cancel button next to the order you wish to cancel. After 24 hours, the order cannot be cancelled.

What are the shipping charges?

+
We charge a flat shipping fee of ₹100 for all orders. Orders above ₹2000 qualify for free shipping. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

+
Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping to international locations. Stay tuned for updates!

What is your return policy?

+
We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. To initiate a return, contact our customer service team.

How long does a refund take?

+
Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.

Is it safe to use my credit card on your site?

+
Yes, absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
